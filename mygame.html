One of the more complicated decisions I had to make was the type of images to use throughout my splash page.
I tried experimenting with high-res photography, but they all felt very static and failed to convey the personality that I wanted the page to have.
I decided to use a more lively & active photo that's representative of the gaming community. Using banners from various games ensures easy recognition, excitement and recognizability.

Wireframes

Initially, I found it difficult to create something "new". After revising a few versions, I realized that it wasn't necessary to create something that hasn't been seen, but to display my information in the most interesting & user friendly way.
Ultimately, I decided that I needed to showcase as much imagery as possible while integrating small pockets of text.
